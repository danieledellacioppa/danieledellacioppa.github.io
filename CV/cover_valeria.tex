\vspace{4pt}

Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in the Customer Service Supervisor role. My previous management experience has given me a strong understanding of what good supervision requires: supporting people, keeping standards consistent, and making decisions that balance excellent customer care with company policies, SOPs and legal requirements.

Although I have not been an agent for very long, I am a quick learner, highly self-motivated and always keen to lead by example. I also understand that when you support agents rather than working only as one, you start to see the wider picture. You become more aware of the challenges people face day to day, as well as the limitations and opportunities within the business.

A lot of my previous roles have involved leading and supporting teams, including my work as a Restaurant Manager, my eight years as an Associate Team Leader at Whole Foods, and my experience as a teacher. Supporting people comes naturally to me. I enjoy creating a positive environment where everyone feels included, trusted and part of the team.

Organisation has always been a big part of my work. I have regularly planned the day, assigned tasks, balanced workloads, kept processes up to date and managed several priorities at once. As an Assistant Manager, while handling responsibilities such as placing orders and monitoring operations, I also made sure the team had clear objectives and understood what was expected of them. I have found that when people know their priorities, the whole team performs better.

I am also comfortable taking ownership of escalations and complex cases. As a Duty Manager, I was often responsible for the whole shop and had to deal with challenging situations where calm judgement and clear decision-making were essential. I am confident de-escalating situations and looking for solutions that work for both the customer and the business.

My experience has also taught me the value of being a reliable support to senior leaders. Having worked as an Associate Team Leader for eight years, I understand the importance of being a second pair of eyes, stepping up when needed and helping the Market Leader keep things running smoothly. I have carried out one-to-one reviews, monitored performance and developed systems to track daily tasks and product knowledge. Constructive feedback, coaching and leading by example have always been important parts of my approach.

Customer service can be demanding, especially during busy periods, so team wellbeing matters. I care about creating an environment where people feel supported, valued and trusted, while still maintaining high standards and compliance with SOPs, GDPR, consumer rights and brand values. I would bring that same warm, practical and consistent approach to this role.

Thank you for considering my application. I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my experience in team leadership, operations and customer service could support the team.

\vspace{4pt}

Kind regards,

\textbf{Valeria Kierbel}
